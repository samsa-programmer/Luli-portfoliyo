My Service

Graphic Designing

Graphic design is a creative and visual communication discipline that involves using various elements, such as typography, imagery, color, and layout, to convey messages and information. It encompasses the creation of visual materials for both digital and print media, including logos, websites, advertisements, brochures, packaging, and more.

3D Art

In the realm of digital art, 3D artists use specialized software such as Autodesk Maya, Blender, or 3ds Max to model, texture, rig, and animate objects or characters in a virtual environment. They employ techniques like polygonal modeling, sculpting, UV mapping, and shader creation to bring their creations to life. The resulting 3D models can be used in various industries, including film, gaming, architecture, product design, and virtual reality.

Video Editing/Vfx

Video editing and visual effects: the art of crafting captivating visuals, combining footage and digital magic to create unforgettable experiences in film and media.
